⚠️ Important Notes

Legal & Safety

Emulators and firmware tools are generally legal to download and use for personal research. Copyrighted ROMs, BIOS files, and commercial software generally are not. Pentesting tools should only be used on systems you own or on systems for which you have explicit written authorization. This links page is for personal reference and educational use.
